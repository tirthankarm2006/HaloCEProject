A remake of the silent cartographer mission of Halo Combat Evolved with cutscenes.
The game ends there after the mission ends.

Tech Stacks for the engine(C++):-
OpenGL(modern OpenGL Glad), GLFW, Assimp, Bullet Physics, OpenAL for sound, GLM for maths, imgui for gui, stb_image(for texture loading)

14/04/2025:- Added Window Classes using GLFW and Glad
